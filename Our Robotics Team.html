Our Robot

Our robot is designed to combine sensor input with motor control. It uses three Sharp sensors to detect obstacles, a servo motor for movement, and a TT motor for driving. All powered by an Arduino Nano and controlled through the TB6612FNG motor driver. A voltage regulator and 11.1V battery supply stable power.
